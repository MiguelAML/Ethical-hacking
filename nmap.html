Nmap

Nmap is a tool used to scan networks and discover devices and services.

Ports are like doors on a computer. Each open port shows a service running. Knowing open ports helps find possible weaknesses. 

nmap -sV 192.168.1.0/24

22/tcp open ssh
80/tcp open http

Port 22 means SSH is running. Port 80 means a website is running. A tester uses this to decide what to investigate next.
