Ethical Hacking

What is Ethical Hacking?

Ethical hacking means testing systems for security problems with permission. The goal is to find weaknesses before real attackers do.

Ethical vs Illegal Hacking

The biggest difference is authorization. Ethical hackers have permission. Illegal hackers do not. Even if they use the same tools, doing it without permission is a crime.

Why Companies Hire Ethical Hackers

Organizations hire ethical hackers to test their security, protect data, and prevent attacks. They simulate real threats in a controlled and legal way.
